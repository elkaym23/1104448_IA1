Theme of the Website

The design was inspired by a game that I like to play, League of Legends, which uses a dark navy-blue color palette with gold accents throughout its interface. I wanted to bring that same look and feel to my site because I liked how sleek it is, and since I usually go for lighter colors (pink is my favorite), I wanted to try something more universal this time. The dark background helps keep things grounded, while the gold borders and highlights give it a clean, polished finish. It’s a style I connect with personally, and I felt it would help my portfolio stand out while still looking professional.

The city lights background also felt like a natural fit. It ties into the gold tones on the site and adds energy to the overall design.
